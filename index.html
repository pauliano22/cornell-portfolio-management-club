Cornell Portfolio Management

About Us

The Cornell Portfolio Management Club (CPMC) is a student-led organization dedicated to providing members with a comprehensive understanding of investment strategies, portfolio management, and the financial markets.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students through hands-on experience, workshops, and networking opportunities with industry professionals. We believe in fostering a collaborative environment where students can learn, share ideas, and build lasting relationships that will aid them in their careers. Join us to enhance your financial acumen and gain valuable skills that will set you apart in the competitive world of finance.
