Cornell Portfolio Management

About Us

The Cornell Portfolio Management Club (CPMC) is a student-led organization dedicated to providing members with a comprehensive understanding of investment strategies, portfolio management, and the financial markets.

Our Mission

The Cornell Portfolio Management Club's mission is to empower students with practical investment knowledge and hands-on portfolio management experience. Through our extensive new member education program, we strive to bridge the gap between academic theory and professional practice, preparing our members for successful careers in finance and investment management.
