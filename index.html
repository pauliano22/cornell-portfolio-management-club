Cornell Portfolio Management Club

About Us

The Cornell Portfolio Management Club (CPMC) is a student-led organization dedicated to providing members with a comprehensive understanding of investment strategies, portfolio management, and the financial markets.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip students with practical investment knowledge and hands-on experience. We bridge the gap between academic theory and professional practice, preparing members for successful careers in finance.

What We Offer

Investment Education

We provide in-depth educational resources and workshops focused on investment strategies, financial analysis, and portfolio management. Our goal is to equip our members with the tools they need to make informed investment decisions.

Portfolio Management Experience

Through hands-on projects and real-world simulations, we give our members the opportunity to manage actual portfolios. This experience allows students to apply what they have learned in a practical and impactful way.

Networking Opportunities

We host events such as speaker sessions, networking mixers, and career panels where students can meet professionals in the finance industry. These events allow our members to connect with top executives, financial analysts, and recruiters from prestigious firms.

Resume Building & Career Development

We offer resume workshops, mock interviews, and career development sessions tailored to those pursuing careers in finance. We help our members refine their resumes, improve interview skills, and increase their job prospects in finance.
