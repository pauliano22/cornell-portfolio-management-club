Welcome!

Expectations

As a Quantitative Developer for CPMC, you will be expected to attend weekly meetings and contribute to a semester-long project.

Meetings

At meetings we will provide guidance for your projects, learn with eachother, and challenge you to think outside the box. We will provide food and may occasionally have guests from the industry.

Learning

We strongly encourage members to learn programming languages like C++, Rust, and Python. We will explore many Machine Learning libraries available to you, which we will provide online resources for.
